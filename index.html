AMBER FOUNDATION

Creating Waves of Goodness: Be a force for positive change with us.

At Amber Foundation, we are committed to making a significant difference in the lives of impoverished communities in North India. Our charity foundation is dedicated to empowering the underprivileged by addressing their fundamental needs for a better life.
Our core mission revolves around improving the quality of life for those who face challenges due to poverty and lack of resources. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to basic necessities that can uplift them and pave the way for a brighter future.
We believe in the strength of unity and collective action. Together, we can create a wave of positive change and empower the lives of countless people in North India. Join us in our journey to make a lasting difference and inspire hope for a brighter tomorrow.

Our Areas of Focus

At Amber Foundation, we firmly believe that every small step towards empowerment can lead to significant transformations. Together, we can build a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential

Living Matters

We understand the dire conditions faced by many families in North India. Amber Foundation strives to provide safe and hygienic living environments, offering shelter and comfort to those who are without a home.

Sweet Water

Access to clean and safe drinking water is a basic human right. We work tirelessly to ensure that communities have access to clean water sources, promoting better health and preventing waterborne diseases.

Education

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Our foundation believes in the transformative power of education and is dedicated to establishing educational institutions in underserved areas. By providing quality education, we aim to equip the next generation with the tools they need to build a brighter future.

Homes

We strive to create a sense of belonging and security for those who have been marginalized or displaced. Through our housing initiatives, we aim to provide stable and sustainable living spaces for families and individuals in need.
